ChangeBug Internship

May - July 2016

I interned at ChangeBug as a marketing and communications intern. ChangeBug is a social enterprise that acts as a pre-incubator for other social enterprises. During my three months there, I wrote marketing collateral and revamped ChangeBug’s website to improve its user engagement. I also started ChangeBug’s social outreach on Facebook and Instagram, to increase brand awareness. I was also seized the opportunity to handle the logistics budget for ChangeBug’s very first social hackathon held in collaboration with YouthCorps Singapore, which aimed to help participants ideate and assess the impact of their social enterprise ideas. On the business development end, I drafted proposals for potential partnerships with corporates for their corporate social responsibility programme.
