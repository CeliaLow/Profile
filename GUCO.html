GUCO Consulting Internship

May - July 2018

GUCO Consulting Group offers digital marketing services that are customised to its clients' needs and budget. During my 3-month internship at GUCO Consulting, I was given the autonomy to plan, implement, and optimise a few campaigns for Castilla, an upmarket furniture brand, and managed digital marketing for Castilla across various channels. I devised a marketing action plan for Castilla’s campaigns, and created content for Castilla’s Facebook page and email campaigns, while handling Castilla’s search engine marketing as well. I also helped to set up Castilla’s new website, and did content marketing for Castilla’s website and blog. On the traditional end, I designed banners to boost walk-in sales, and also reached out to corporates for potential partnership opportunities. In order to optimise the campaign strategies, I collated reports for Castilla’s digital channels, and looked for ways to improve metrics through testing and new initiatives.
