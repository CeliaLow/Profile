SUTD Research Internship

Nov 2014 - Jan 2015

I interned at the Singapore University of Technology and Design for a 2-month research project on the characterisation of urinary metabolites as a way to detect colorectal cancer. My partner and I collated a report and presented our findings at a school Science Symposium. Through this project, we learnt how to adapt to the challenges and uncertainty faced, while maintaining integrity throughout.
